Section 2

In 2019, the NYU Public Safety Lab began collecting daily individual-level jail records from more than 1,000 publicly available jail rosters in the United States as part of its Jail Data Initiative. This website provides dashboard reports on these records, aggregated to the facility/day, as well as access to individual jail rosters, bookings and associated court records.
